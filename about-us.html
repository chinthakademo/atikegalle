Who we are?

     The Advanced Technological Institute of Kegalle is governed by the Sri Lanka Institute of Advanced Technological Education. ATI Kegalle was established on the 29th of January 2009, with a view of catering for the requirements of higher education of the qualified and eligible students in the Kegalle District.

    Initially, the institute was established as an ATI section at the premises of Warakapola Technical College in the year 1995. At the commencement of the ATI section, Higher National Diplomas in Accountancy and English were offered to the students. The year 2009 is marked as a crucial juncture in the journey of ATI Kegalle as it received a separate premise. Thus ATI Kegalle was erected in a calm and serene ambience in the epicenter of Sabaragamuwa Province. The Institute, now being operated with a large number of dynamic and ambitious students enrolled each year and a well-qualified staff guiding students for the enhancement of the skills as well as knowledge, is heading towards excellence in professional education. Apart from HNDA and HNDE courses which had earned a great demand among students, the introduction of the HNDIT course simultaneous to the erection of new premises at Kegalle has rendered opportunities for the students to open up their career paths in Information Technology.

    Moreover, the ATI Kegalle premises comprises rich physical resources of fully equipped computer laboratories, a library and comfortable classroom facilities that facilitate the students to carry out their studies in a friendly environment. Accordingly, ATI Kegalle leads the path as the perfect platform for molding professionals who unleash multi-disciplinary talents in the fields of Accountancy, English and Information Technology.
